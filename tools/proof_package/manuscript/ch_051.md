# Chapter 51: The Symphony of Scars

Kael was still, a statue in the heart of the ice-cave. He sat with Dawnbreaker laid across his knees, not in meditation, but in listening. The voice from his dream—no, from his bones—was not a command, but a quiet, resonant hum. It was the sound of a bowstring drawn taut, of a moment before a fall. It did not speak of destiny; it spoke of geometry.
