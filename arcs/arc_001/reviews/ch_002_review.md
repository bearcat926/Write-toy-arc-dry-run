## Observations and Flags

### 1. Key Warmth - MINOR (Needs tracking)
Ch_002 describes the key as "warm now, almost hot" and "pulsing faintly against her skin like a second heartbeat." Ch_001 made no mention of the key having warmth or magical properties—only that it was "black iron, ornate and old."

**Assessment**: Not a contradiction. The warmth likely developed after Elara touched the glowing letter and experienced the magical awakening at the end of ch_001. However, this should be tracked as an evolving property for future chapters.

### 2. Partnership Duration - FLAG FOR TRACKING
"In six years of working together" establishes a specific timeline for the Elara-Maren partnership. This should be recorded in character ledgers to prevent future contradictions.

### 3. Maren's Alchemical Compound - CONSISTENT BUT EXPANDED
Ch_002 provides new detail about Maren's vial: "when exposed to air, it produced a thick, acrid smoke that burned the eyes and lungs. Effective for escape. Less effective for interrogation." This expands on ch_001's brief mention without contradicting it.

### 4. License Renewal - NEW DETAIL
Elara states her license was "renewed three months ago." This is new information that could be relevant if the Reformation Council's inquiry becomes a plot point. No contradiction.

### 5. Courier's Tattoo - PLOT DEVELOPMENT
The serpent tattoo on the courier connects him to the letter/key symbolism. This is a deliberate plot development, not a continuity error.
