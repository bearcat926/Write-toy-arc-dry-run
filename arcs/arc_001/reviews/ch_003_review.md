## Minor Observations (Non-blocking)

1. **Elara's childhood testing**: New information that she was tested and found to have "slight sensitivity to ink compounds" - This is a reasonable addition that actually enhances continuity by explaining why she works with ink/documents. However, it creates an interesting tension: the examiners found "nothing remarkable" but the letter claims she has an unrecognized "gift." This could be intentional dramatic irony or a plot point about Archive examiners concealing her true abilities.

2. **Maren's crossbow position**: In ch_001, it was "strapped beneath her jacket." In ch_002, she had a crossbow visible at the door. In ch_003, she sleeps with it "across her chest." This is not a contradiction (different situations) but worth tracking for consistency in how Maren carries and accesses her weapon.

3. **Callum's physical description**: "Silver beard and his cold, assessing eyes" is new in ch_003. No prior physical description existed, so no conflict, but should be noted as established canon going forward.

4. **"The gift" interpretation**: Elara interprets "the gift" as potentially a magical talent ("something in me. The letter said I had a gift I haven't understood"). The letter's wording ("She trusted you with a gift you have not yet fully understood") is ambiguous - could mean a magical ability, knowledge, or a physical object. Elara's interpretation is reasonable for her character but may not be correct.

5. **Truncated sentence at chapter end**: The final line "She'd come to stand beside Elara, reading over her shoulder" appears to be cut off mid-paragraph. This is likely a drafting artifact, not a continuity issue, but should be addressed in editing.

## Potential Concerns to Watch

1. **The uncle reference**: Ch_001 letter warns "Do not trust the man who calls himself your uncle." No uncle has been mentioned in ch_002 or ch_003. This thread needs to be picked up eventually but is not yet overdue.

2. **Thornfield Gate location**: Neither ch_002 nor ch_003 has established where the Thornfield Gate is or how to find it. With three days until the equinox, this information will need to be discovered.

3. **Callum's knowledge of the letter**: How Callum learned about the letter so quickly remains unexplained. This is likely a deliberate mystery but should be resolved eventually.
