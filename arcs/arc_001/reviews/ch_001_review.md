### ⚠️ FLAG: Young Age for Seal Destruction
Elara claims she destroyed the Ashworth seal, which would have occurred when she was **12 years old**. While not impossible (especially given the suggestion of unusual abilities or circumstances), this requires explanation in future chapters. How did a 12-year-old come to destroy a family seal? Was this connected to her mother's death in the same event?

**Recommendation**: Future chapters should address how Elara, at age 12, was responsible for destroying the seal. This is a significant character detail that needs clarification.
